## Notes
- This is a pre-opening restaurant launching Summer 2026
- Site launches first as a "coming soon" landing page, then expands to full site
- Design aesthetic: dark, moody, warm, luxury. Think candlelit fine dining, not bright casual
- Brand palette is warm and earthy, inspired by desert landscapes, dried chiles, agave, terracotta pottery, and wheat fields
- The aesthetic is warm, textured, and grounded -- not cool/moody. Think golden hour in the Sonoran desert, not candlelit speakeasy
- All backgrounds should feel warm brown/black, not cool charcoal
- The grain/noise texture overlay is even more appropriate now given the textured feel of the brand materials
- Three distinct experiences: Kitchen (wood-fired), Crudo (raw bar), Cantina (mezcal/cocktail bar)
- Interior features: green marble bar top, ribbed glass pendants, scalloped ceiling tiles, terracotta accent walls, dark marble dining tables, backlit bottle wall, sheer curtain room dividers
- Typography note: the brand has custom display fonts (Campground for titles, Badhorse for accents). Until custom font files arrive, Montserrat 300 italic stands in for display. Space Mono is used for subtitles/labels. All navigation and UI text uses Montserrat in small caps or tracked-out uppercase
- Animation philosophy: everything moves slowly. Nothing bounces. Nothing is playful. Motion should feel like candlelight -- organic, warm, unhurried. Easing should favor ease-out for entrances
- Section labels use Space Mono, 11px, weight 400, letter-spacing 4px, uppercase, in the golden wheat color
- The site should feel like walking into the restaurant: dark, warm, layered, unhurried
